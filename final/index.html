Dev year band: Guitar

I'm making an electric guitar out of cardboard, ESP32, breadboard, wired, resistors, buttons, etc.

Arduino

I started by thinking about how to press a button to make a sound, and then went back to thinking about making the first six buttons all specify a sound. The buttons only control the change in sound, and the seventh button controls the playback of the sound.

processing

I made each button represent a sound, labeling the sounds in order. Then consider making a visual, the first step is to show the chord each button represents when I press the first six buttons, then add a circle that changes size based on the sound wave, and finally add random colors that also change color randomly based on the sound wave.

prototype

I referenced a guitar model from a game, then cut it out of cardboard, dug holes in the cardboard, put the feet of the buttons in, and soldered on the WIRE to attach to the breadboard.
